Course Overview

This interactive online class is designed to build and enhance essential skills in reading, writing, math, and science. Perfect for students who want to improve their academic performance and understanding of core concepts.

Subjects Covered

Reading:

  • Comprehension skills
  • Vocabulary building
  • Analyzing stories and texts

Writing:

  • Grammar and sentence structure
  • Creative writing and essays

Math:

Science:
